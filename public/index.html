Automated Bus Scheduling and Route Management System

Welcome to the Automated Bus Scheduling and Route Management System for the Delhi Transport Corporation. This system helps streamline the scheduling of buses and route management, making public transport more efficient.

Problem Statement:

Managing and scheduling buses across various routes is a challenging task, especially in a city like Delhi. This system is designed to automate the process of assigning buses and crew members to different routes based on various factors such as availability, time, and routes.
